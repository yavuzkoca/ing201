Thesis: In spite of the fact that AI technologies contains some risks that may even be caused to end of humanity, 
	it should be supported because it is increasing welfare by developing daily and work life, also minimazing
	some risks like lack of security or low productivity.

I. Can be risky
	A. Unethical behaviours
		1. Racisim (Anupam Chandler,The Racist Algorithm,2016,p26) 
		2. Mocking (Diane Proudfoot,Mocking AI Panic,2015)
			   (Sam Shead,2017)
	B. Possible dangerous consequences
		1. Destroy humanity (Elon musk,The Guardian,2018,p1)
		2. Restrain humanity (S.Hawking,Southland Times,2014,p6)

II. Increases welfare 				
	A. Daily life					
		1. Education (Dyllan Furness, 2017)							
		2. Medical (Sammour,Cohen...,2017,p870)
	B. Work life			
		1. End of modern slavery (Chris Weller,2017)
		2. New Jobs (STAMFORD,December 13, 2017)
III. Minimizes risks
	A. Security
		1. Work life(Johnston,Phillips...,2004)
		2. Daily life(Bradshaw-Martin, Easton, 2014)
	B. Productivity
		1. Energy (Jucikas, 2017)
		2. Economy (Fethi, 2010, p. 190)
